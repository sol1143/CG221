Who We Are

a non-profit organization based on encouraging girls to code

Dedicated to closing the gender gap in this growing technological industry, we encourage young learn the language of coding.

The Problem

Girls in the technology field are being left behind

Technology jobs are among the fastest growing in the country, yet girls are being left behind. Girls between the ages of 13-17 are most likely to lose interest, it is our mission to prevent this drop off.
