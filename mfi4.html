My Favorite Internet


www.flightradar24.com

This website tracks the different on-going flights all around the world. It's quite amazing how many on-going flights there really are every day/every minute. You don't realize how many flights there actually are flying above us. With this site, now you do!


www.nadjozna.wixsite.com/nadjozna

This website is very uniquely designed. It isn't a typically structured page and seems as if it was designed by a brush. This unique element shows me the development of web design. Most sites seem as if they are grid-like and highly structured, although this one shows me the more artistic side of web-design.
